enum GamePhase {
    OPENING = 0,
    MIDDLE_EARLY,
    MIDDLE,         // both sides castled by now
    ENDGAME,
    ENDGAME_LATE
};


   ANd shouldnt middlegames also (not just openeing and early middlegame) take castling into account?


Pawns dont matter unless specified otherwise

If both sides castled, it must be middlegame or later. Promote as needed. 

If the enemy has only a minor piece, or nothing, (enemy has no major pieces) then its ENDGAME_LATE

If no major pieces, enemy or friend, then it must be ENDGAME or later. Promote as needed.

if no major pieces have left the board, then it cnnot be ENDGAME or ENDGAME_LATE. Demote as needed.

If no queens, then it must be MIDDLE or later. Promote as needed.

The sum of all minor pieces (enemy and freind) on the board is now called "mm". 
AT beggining of the game, mm=8. This is the mm rules . mm rules are OVERRIDDEN by the above rules.
    mm=8    OPENING
    mm=7    OPENING
    mm=6    MIDDLE_EARLY
    mm=5    MIDDLE_EARLY
    mm=4    MIDDLE              2 pieces gone per side
    mm=3    MIDDLE
    mm=2    ENDGAME
    mm=1    ENDGAME_LATE
    mm=0    ENDGAME_LATE
